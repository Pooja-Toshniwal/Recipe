Gingerbear Cookies

Cute and delicious



Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/3 cup firmly packed dark brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup white sugar, plus more for rolling
  • 1/3 cup unsulphured molasses
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

Steps

  1. Line two cookie sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter, brown sugar, and 1/3 cup white sugar until light and fluffy. Add in the 1/3 cup molasses, egg, and vanilla and mix until smooth.
  3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  4. To make the filling, place white chocolate, heavy cream, 1/2 teaspoon molasses, pinch of ginger, pinch of cinnamon,
    pinch of nutmeg, and pinch of salt into a small microwave-safe bow
  5. Allow filling to set up until soft set, about 15 minutes.
  6. Place black Candy Melts into a small microwave-safe bowl, and microwave according to package directions
  7. Pour melted Candy Melts into a piping bag, and snip a small hole in the end of it.
  8. Pipe on the bears' eyes and noses; reheating the Candy Melts as necessary.
  9. Allow the eyes and noses to set before serving
